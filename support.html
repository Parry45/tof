SCHOLARS (The 2025 TOF Scholars Cohort)

This year, we are launching the first-ever TOF Scholars Cohort. Our goal is to fully or partially fund the tuition and relocation costs of up to five exceptional Ghanaians who earn places at top global universities

These are not just scholarships. They are investments in builders, makers, and leaders.

From engineering to law, public health to business, these students will return equipped with the tools, vision, and global experience to create transformation on the continent.

What Your Support Covers

  • Tuition for up to one year of study

  • Flights and visa costs

  • Accommodation and living expenses

  • Professional development opportunities

  • Scholar programming, mentorship, and alumni retreats

Every donation writes a new story. Every cedi or pound raised brings us closer to the world we want to live in—one where no dream ends at the door.
